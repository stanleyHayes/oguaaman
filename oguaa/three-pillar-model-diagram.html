The three-pillar model

Everything on the platform is one of three things — a person, a body, or a piece of content — and they all sit on one shared foundation.

Pillar 1
Members
People. The identity layer — who you are, the town and institutions you rep.
residentsold studentsartistsoffice-holders
Pillar 2
Institutions
The bodies. Official, verified profiles — with offices held by members.
schoolstraditional councilsassociationsfaith & civic bodies
Pillar 3
Listings
The content. Every contributed entry, one shared lifecycle.
businessesmemorialseventsmemoriesopportunities

Members→Listings

The shared foundation
One submit → review → publish engine
plus accounts, verification & moderation — built once, used by all three pillars
accountsmoderation queueverificationnotificationsscheduled remembrances

The payoff: every new feature is either a new listing type, a profile attribute, an institution kind, or a view over data that already exists — not a new system.
